Thank you for using my Car Buying Calculator program. This program is designed residents of Wisconsin only. You will need the following information handy:

- Year, make, and model of your car
- Power source of your car (gas, hybrid, or electric)
- The county where the car will be stored
- Any applicable tax exemption documents
- The sticker price of the car
- Any trade-in values and/or down payments
